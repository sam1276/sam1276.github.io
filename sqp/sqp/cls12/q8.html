Section A 1 Marks

Consider the statements given below and then choose the correct output from the given options:

pride="#G20 Presidency"
print(pride[-2:2:-2])

a. ndsr

b. ceieP0

c. ceieP

d. yndsr

c. ceieP

`pride="#G20 Presidency"`: Assigns the string "#G20 Presidency" to the variable `pride`.
`pride[-2:2:-2]`: Slicing from the second-to-last character ("c") to the second character ("e") in reverse order with a step of -2.
Output: The selected characters are "ce", and then "P" is added as it is. The final output is "ceieP".
