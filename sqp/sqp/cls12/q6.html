Question Number 6

Section A 1 Marks

Riya wants to transfer pictures from her mobile phone to her laptop. She uses Bluetooth Technology to connect two devices. Which type of network will be formed in this case?

a. PAN

b. LAN

c. MAN

d. WAN

Answer
a. PAN
Explanation

Riya's Bluetooth connection creates a PAN (Personal Area Network), allowing her to transfer pictures between her phone and laptop within a short range.
